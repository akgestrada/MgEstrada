Budget Phones

New doesn't always mean better.

Used/Old Phones

Flagships from ~2 years ago are almost better at every way than budget phones now, BUT

Software Updates

Flagship Phones usually have 4 years of sofware updates. If you buy a flagship phone from 2 years ago, you will be losing 2 years of software updates.

Reliability

Battery degrades over time, even when it is not used.

Scam

Are you sure that the seller isn't hiding any problems about the phone? Did the previous owner change/repair any parts of the phone? Did the seller manage to burn the screen? Is the phone even the real one or a copy? Did the phone have any problems before (recalling the phone) but the owner wasn't able to return it. Did the owner change the phone

Availability

Where are you going to buy the phone? Most retailers, even after years, do not reduce the price of the phone. That is a problem because the reason you are even buying a used/old phone is that you want to get the phone for a lower price.
